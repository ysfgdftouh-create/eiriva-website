FAQ

What most travelers ask before booking.

Who performs the surgeries?

Only board‑certified surgeons at accredited facilities. We are a coordination platform — we don’t perform medical acts.

What’s included in a package?

Pre‑op assessment, surgery fees, anesthesia, hospital stay (if needed), hotel nights, private transfers, and on‑site assistance.

Can I bring a companion?

Yes. We can arrange twin rooms and add companion transfers and meals to your plan.
